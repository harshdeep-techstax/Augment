Frequently Asked Questions

  • I work full-time, is this program for me?

    We know you’re busy. That’s why we designed the Augment MBA Program to work around the schedule of working professionals, and not the other way around. At Augment, we believe that business education should be adapted to the lives of people that work.


  • Is Augment accredited?

    No, and we have no intention of seeking accreditation. An accredited master’s degree comes with many constraints. That’s everything we’re working against. We do however provide graduates with our Linkedin-ready certificate signed by Chris Barton, the Founder of Shazam.  


  • An MBA for $1750? Whats the catch?

    Our independence from official academic institutions and cumbersome accreditation criteria allows us to have a completely different business model from other MBA programs. You’re just paying for an education, not all the expensive bureaucracy that surrounds traditional MBAs.


  • How long will it take me to complete the program?


    There are currently 20 hours of class time, with new modules coming soon. Because the program is self-paced, completion times differ from one student to another. Average completion time stands around 2-3 months. 


  • Is Augment fully online?

    Yes. The Augment Business School is 100% online. You can access our program from any device, anywhere, and at any time.


  • Can I get a refund if I’m not satisfied?

    Absolutely. All of our students are eligible for a full refund within 15 days of enrollment - no questions asked.


  • Does Augment offer financial aid?

    We are currently designing a Merit-Based Scholarship Program for highly motivated individuals who lack the financial resources to access our program. For scholarship inquiries contact us at team@augment.school


  • Is the Augment MBA just video lessons ?

    No. Our cinematic lessons are only one component of the program, which also includes quizzes, case studies, written lessons, downloadable handbooks and community networking.


  • Can I pay in instalments?

    Yes. Payment can be broken down in 5 monthly instalments of $350.

  • Does Augment offer group discounts?


    Yes. For group or B2B inquiries, contact us at team@augment.school.
